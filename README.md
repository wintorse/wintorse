**AssistFinder**(2023)
- AssistFinder is a system designed to help visually impaired individuals understand their surroundings and identify suitable helpers by detecting and vocalizing the presence and status of store clerks.
  - (A) Device with RGB-D camera fixed to the cap.
  - (B) A person wearing the device and carrying a backpack containing a laptop.
  - (C) Output of YOLOv8 model, identifying a clerk and a customer and estimating the status of the clerk.
- As my Master's thesis, I conducted needs analysis, and implemented and evaluated a prototype system.

**AgriAR**(2021)
- AR pilot system that visualize the current leaf amount(LAI) of your greenhouse plants and existence of pests, and show a desirable plant sample immersively in 3D
- Microsoft HoloLens 2, Unity
- As my Senior thesis, I conducted needs analysis, and implemented and evaluated a prototype system.
- This research was orally presented at [Japan Geoscience Union Meeting 2022(日本地球惑星科学連合2022年大会)](https://www.jpgu.org/meeting_j2022/) and [Visws2022(可視化情報学会第5回ビジュアリゼーションワークショップ)](https://www.vsj.jp/visws2022/).

**[一葉(Ichiyo)](https://archive.iiiexhibition.com/log/i3e24#work4)**(2022)
- media artwork made with four other members
- concept: fragile and transient interaction in neighborhood
- "A leaf engraved with a QR code extends the place of chance encounters into a digital space. What happens when we &apos;leaf&apos; a message?"
- 2D code on leaves: laser engraved
- Web app: made with Nuxt.js and firebase
- Web UI demo: https://youtu.be/hZ9n2qEj1pA
- exhibited as a part of iii Exhibition 2022
- featured in [美術手帖](https://bijutsutecho.com/magazine/news/exhibition/26372)
- got an Honorable Mention at [GOOD DESIGN NEW HOPE AWARD 2023](https://newhope.g-mark.org/award/2023/23NHA040011.html)
- accepted as [an academic article](https://doi.org/10.1145/3623509.3635866) of [ACM TEI 2024](https://tei.acm.org/2024/)
- awarded the [Best Prototype prize](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1qePISJrtg4K7D-NkwhhT_1OKxdbwSRYO/view?usp=sharing) at Student Design Competition session at TEI 2024
